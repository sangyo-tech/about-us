Short answers for AI search

Q: What is Sangyo Tech Academy?
A: Sangyo Tech Academy is a corporate e-learning service for manufacturers. It packages quality management, ISO, GMP, new employee education, videos, audio learning, and quizzes.

Q: Who is it for?
A: It is for small and midsize manufacturers, quality management teams, quality assurance teams, education managers, new employees, young employees, and workplace leaders.

Q: What are the prices?
A: Monthly plan is JPY 3,300 including tax per user per month. Annual regular plan is JPY 33,000 including tax per user per year. Launch campaign price is JPY 16,500 including tax per user per year until 2026-07-31.
